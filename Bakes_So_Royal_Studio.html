♛ Add this app to your phone

📱 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open this page in Safari (not inside email or another app).
  2. Tap the Share icon — the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add (top right). The Bakes So Royal icon appears on your home screen.
  5. Always open it from that icon — your orders and settings stay saved.

If you only have the file (not a web link): save it to the Files app first, then in Files tap the file → Share → look in the grey row for Open in Safari. Easiest of all is to open it from a normal web link.

🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen → Add.
